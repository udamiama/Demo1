Dogs in Sri Lanka

    German Shepherd

German Shepherds are famous for their intelligent, loyal, and hardworking natures. They are often used as guide, therapy, search-and-rescue, police, military, and narcotics and explosives detecting dogs. While they can come across as aloof to strangers, they are known to be wonderful family dogs.

Rottweiler

Big, muscular and strong, the Rottweiler is loyal and has a natural guarding instinct. They need human contact, good training and firm, consistent handling from birth – they must always know who's boss. Rottweilers also need a large amount of space and exercise – they are playful and enjoy chasing games.

Bullmastiff

Bullmastiffs are great watchdogs and great for families. Bullmastiffs are domestic guard dogs from the 19th century. Gamekeepers used these dogs to protect estates. They are a mix of the English Mastiff and Old English Bulldog, later recognized as purebred dogs by the Kennel Club.

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is considered an intelligent, gentle natured and very affectionate breed of dog. As is typical with retriever breeds, the breed is generally calm and biddable, being very easy to train and extremely keen to please their master.

Cocker Spaniel

The Cocker is the AKC's smallest sporting spaniel, standing about 14 to 15 inches. The coat comes in enough colors and patterns to please any taste. The well-balanced body is sturdy and solid, and these quick, durable gundogs move with a smooth, easy gait
